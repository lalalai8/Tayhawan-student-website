Welcome to Tayhawan!

Tayhawan is a peaceful rural community in the municipality of Lezo, Aklan. We pride ourselves on our unity, cooperation, and the preservation of our local traditions.

Our Barangay Information System is designed to provide residents with easy access to community news, services, and official records.

Our History

The name Tayhawan originated during the Spanish colonial period. It was born from a simple misunderstanding when a local resident answered "Tay Juan" (That is Juan) to a Spanish soldier's question.

Mission

To educate, unite, and mobilize the people toward natural farming and entrepreneurship through collaborative effort and active participation.

Vision

A premier productive farming and progressive healthy community empowered by good governance and collective decision-making.
